LEGO Mario Codes

Structure

Each LEGO Mario barcode consists of five unique colors that are then repeated as a sequence:
  • Green
  • Red
  • Blue
  • Lime
  • Pink
  • Violet
  • Yellow
  • Teal
The barcode always seems to start with Green, Blue, or Teal, and then Red on an edge, so the last three are the only significant ones.
